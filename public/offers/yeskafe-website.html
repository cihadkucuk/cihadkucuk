Our Story

A sunlit atelier
by the river

Founded by Ye Qian, a wine educator and one of Prague's leading voices in the low-intervention wine scene, Ye's Kafe is more than a café. It's an airy, glass-fronted retreat where carefully curated natural wines meet exceptional specialty coffee — steps from the Vltava in the heart of Karlín.

The shelves are lined with bottles from small Czech and European producers. The space doubles as a wine shop and intimate tasting room — warm, thoughtful, unforgettable.
