Meet Your Surgeon

A Surgeon Who Truly Listens

With over 15 years of specialist experience in plastic and aesthetic surgery, Dr. Zuzana Černá has built her practice on a foundation of clinical precision and genuine patient connection. Having trained at leading European institutions, she brings a depth of expertise that is reflected in every outcome she achieves.

As a female surgeon, Dr. Černá understands her patients from a uniquely personal perspective. Her consultations are characterised by warmth, honesty, and time — she will never rush a decision, and she will always prioritise results that look natural, not operated on. Patients frequently comment on feeling genuinely heard, and on the sense of safety and care that defines the Laderma experience.

Laderma clinic operates to full EU medical standards with an on-site anaesthesiology team and private recovery rooms. Dr. Černá is fluent in both Czech and English and regularly welcomes international patients from the UK, Ireland, Germany, Scandinavia, and beyond. All pre- and post-operative documentation is provided in English.

Frequently Asked Questions

Everything You Need to Know

Yes — Dr. Černá and the entire Laderma team are fully fluent in English. All consultations, pre-operative instructions, post-operative documentation, and follow-up communications are provided in clear English. We also assist German-speaking patients in their native language.
Once you book, we provide a comprehensive Prague guide including recommended hotels situated close to our Vinohrady clinic. Prague is extremely well-connected — it is just 2 to 2.5 hours by direct flight from London, Dublin, Frankfurt, Stockholm, Oslo, and Copenhagen. Our patient coordinator is happy to advise on transfers and logistics.
Your quoted price includes the surgeon's fee, anaesthesiologist fee, operating theatre, nursing care, post-operative garments, in-clinic follow-up appointments, and your dedicated patient coordinator. Accommodation and flights are arranged separately — we will guide you to the best options for your stay.
Recovery times vary by procedure. As a general guide: eyelid surgery typically requires a 4–5 day stay; breast and body procedures 5–7 days; and facelift patients should plan for 7–10 days. Dr. Černá will provide a detailed timeline specific to your procedure at consultation.
We operate a comprehensive remote aftercare programme for all international patients. This includes scheduled video follow-up consultations at 1 week, 1 month, and 3 months post-surgery. You will also have direct WhatsApp and email access to your dedicated patient coordinator throughout your recovery. Full documentation is provided for your GP or local healthcare provider.
